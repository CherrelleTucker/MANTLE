# Naming History

How we landed on **MANTLE**.

---

## The chosen name

**MANTLE** — *Manual, Acronyms, Notes, Transition, Logistics, Engagement*

The cultural phrase *"passing the mantle"* is exactly what happens at every Project Coordinator handoff. The name does communication work the moment someone hears it — no explanation needed.

---

## Constraints

- **Must not collide with a NASA mission name.** NASA's naming office reserves mission names; using one for an internal tool creates confusion and can be rejected.
- **Must be easy to say and spell.**
- **Should fit the metaphor of the platform's purpose** (transition, knowledge handoff, continuity).
- **Should align loosely with Barrios's brand themes** (mission-driven, partnership, heritage, "passionately advancing humanity").
- **Backronym-friendly** — letters should map naturally to platform content without forcing.

---

## Alternatives considered

### Eliminated due to NASA mission collision
| Name | Mission collision |
|------|-------------------|
| PACE | NASA Earth-observing satellite (Plankton, Aerosol, Cloud, ocean Ecosystem) |
| ATLAS | ULA rocket family + multiple NASA missions |
| RELAY | Early NASA communications satellite (1960s) |
| HORIZON | New Horizons probe |
| COURIER | Courier 1B early US comm satellite |

### Considered, ranked alongside MANTLE
| Name | Metaphor | Why not chosen |
|------|----------|----------------|
| **CAIRN** | Trail markers stacked by predecessors so successors find the way | Excellent metaphor (asynchronous, additive, multi-generational) but requires explanation; MANTLE is more immediately legible |
| **COMPASS** | Guidance, orientation | Common, slightly corporate; less distinctive than MANTLE |
| **BRIDGE** | Connects departing to arriving | Solid but generic |
| **ANCHOR** | Stability through change | Reasonable but less specific to handoff |
| **LEGACY** | Heritage and preservation | Strong fit with Barrios's 45-year emphasis, but "Y" is a hard letter to backronym |
| **NEXUS** | Connection point | Slightly cold/technical; less human than MANTLE |
| **TRELLIS** | A structure that supports growth | Interesting but obscure |

---

## Backronym derivation

Letters chosen to map cleanly to the actual content stored in MANTLE:

| Letter | Word | Maps to |
|--------|------|---------|
| **M** | Manual | The cookbook export and recurring task playbooks |
| **A** | Acronyms | The Acronyms Glossary List |
| **N** | Notes | Personal annotations and Decisions Log |
| **T** | Transition | The whole purpose of the platform |
| **L** | Logistics | Travel, badging, room booking, reporting |
| **E** | Engagement | Stakeholders List and meeting catalog |

No forced letters. Each component corresponds to a real piece of the data model.
