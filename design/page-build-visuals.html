WHAT MANTLE IS

A platform for coordinator-to-coordinator knowledge transfer

PCs leave, contracts shift, and institutional knowledge vanishes. MANTLE captures relationships, meetings, decisions, and unwritten rules — and packages it for the next person.

Built by coordinators, for coordinators.

Who can see what's on this site

MANTLE is internal to Barrios and your contract team. Stakeholder records, working styles, and decisions are visible to:

  • You
  • Other PCs assigned to the same contract
  • Your Barrios manager
  • MANTLE administrators

Items tagged Confidential are personal observations and never appear in public reports or external slides.

WHY THIS PAGE EXISTS

Your replacement should not be guessing

Whether you are moving to another contract or fully leaving the role, the people you have worked with do not have to be re-learned from scratch.

This page is also for quarterly maintenance. Keep records fresh so when handoff comes, your cookbook is already 80% written.
