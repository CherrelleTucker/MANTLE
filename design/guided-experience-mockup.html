How to work with each person

Knowing each stakeholder's working preferences saves you months of friction. Here are the discovery questions to ask in your first 30 days — and what each person you've already met has shared so far.

Discovery questions for new stakeholders

  • What's the best channel to reach you for non-urgent things?
  • When I'm editing a doc you own, do you want suggestions or direct edits?
  • What hours do you protect — and when's your hard stop?
  • Do you decide in real time, or do you need to think on it overnight?
  • Who else needs to be in the loop when we make a call together?
  • What's the fastest way to lose your trust?
